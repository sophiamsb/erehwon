Introduction

Internet is everywhere and invisible. Everyone has it and almost everybody use it for any purpose. The internet creates in me an insatiable curiosity to dive in and to navigate anything available, I’ve been quickly accustomed to use youtube , various social media platforms in a regular basis which can end to an exhausting feeling of fatigue too. Sometimes I reckon hardly a time without a computer , without my phone, without internet. I feel like I waste my time, the longer I stay on the internet. I’m really busy online. Internet is my daily routine as most of my daily routines are based on it. Whether I’m in the train, at work, on my bike or at home, I’m glued to my phone, I'm sitting in front of my computer all day, questioning google, chatting, sending links, reloading my facebook instagram page, checking mails, listening to music watching tv shows, interviews, movie scenes and many more things.
Every person in the city can access internet, sometimes there is also spots where you can have it for free, it's accessible without much difficulties. It has become difficult to escape the connected world. Before internet resonated with computers, but now it’s not really much the case. Nowadays, most people have at least two connections , a computer and a mobile but it can extends to a smart watch, speakers… many objects , the internet of things stuff.
So why trying to resist this worldwide intensification ?
What is it to be immersed in a digital world ?
Does Internet modify the human nature?

People are nowhere like a fluid living both online and offline realities permanently. Actually, why not suggesting the online/offline separation is over. Now, everything is about consciousness, at least the illusion of it. We are floating avatars, the internet connection opened a reality in which people are needy for connection, hyper-micro-split-fake identities.
The dualistic concept of reality is the most traditional and convincing in our society. It refers to the belief in two kind of reality: material /immaterial -physical/spiritual. Because of this concept, the mind is categorically separated from the body. So when the question appears, we address it to the physical world where our body functions, breathes, and navigates. From there, we believe more in a socially shared physicality, which can be viewable, hearable, touchable, lickable, smellable, and totally experienceable by someone else.In contrast the mental world of thoughts, memories,ideas, fantasies, and imagination is considered ephemeral and ‘unreal’.

The fictional place of Erehwon is being spoken by a virtual avatar. A character on his quest and wonder to find his representation in a new time-space.
The person of Erehwon is a online-human creature, an hybrid mutated version of the old human. His character appears like an avatar, an absent human body.
His avatar has appearances, race, class, genders, and personalities that is in a constant state of flux. Human is being speed it up. A single or several human consciousness being dispersed accross multiple bodies. There is no false consciousness.
